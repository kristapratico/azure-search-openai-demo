Talks: A Fireside Chat With the Hosts of the Core.py Podcast Saturday - May 18th, 2024 5 p.m.-5:30 p.m. in Ballroom A   Presented by:  Łukasz Langa Pablo Galindo Salgado  Description Pablo and Łukasz run a new podcast where they discuss the implementation details of CPython and what the development process looks like. In this fireside chat session with invited core developer guests, they talk about how Python 3.13 reaches the pivotal point for some of the most anticipated features ever. Come join us to hear about how working on Python works in practice. Ask questions, be part of this exciting time in the history of our favorite programming language!
