Talks: Event-Driven asyncio: A Case Study of Trio's API Saturday - May 18th, 2024 10:30 a.m.-11 a.m. in Room 301-305   Presented by:  Junya Fukuda  Description Asyncio is highly efficient in operating within a single thread, making it exceptionally well-suited for environments with limited resources. In our product, we leverage Python's asynchronous capabilities to streamline the decision-making processes of our robots. I will individually introduce trio and trio-util, as well as anyio and asyncio, in the presentation.
