 FlaskCon 2024     Join us in a mini conference dedicated to Flask, its community and ecosystem, as well as related web technologies. Meet maintainers and community members, learn about how to get involved, and join us during the sprint days to contribute. FlaskCon 2024 will be happening Friday, May 17 inside PyCon US 2024. More information can be found on the FlaskCon website, we look forward to seeing you there! https://flaskcon.com/ Organized by David Lord, Phil Jones, Adam Englander, David Carmichael, Abdur-Rahmaan Janhangeer
